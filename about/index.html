Kylen is a freelance illustrator, designer, and web developer living in West Philadelphia.

As a part of the artist collective Yonic Daze, Kylen collaborates with independent artists to build exciting venues for their work to take shape online. With a knack for bringing experimental ideas to life on the web and an enormous resource of collaborators, Kylen manifests projects that are dynamic, expressive, and memorable.

Marked early on with a deep appreciation for community and artistic rituals, Kylen is always working to support her friends and fellow artists, whether that means making a flyer for your next show, acting in your low budget short film, designing the cover of your zine, or helping you pick out your outfit.

Hit her up.
